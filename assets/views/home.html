Find great spots for food...

Halfway between where you and your friends are.

Step 1

You and a friend who works a few blocks away want to grab lunch, but want to meet somewhere between your two offices. Start Cnvrge, which will get your geolocation and provide you a link to email or text your friend. After they click the link and their location is recieved, you will get a list of up to 20 restaraunt suggestions.

Step 2

You both will have the option to click yes or no on the restaurant suggestions. If one or both of you click no, the choice will be discarded and you will get another suggestion. If you both click yes, you will recieve the address of the location.

Step 3

Once you get the address, you can click on it to open it in Google maps. And that's it! The app is simple, and will help you get to your lunch sooner. No need to dig through pages of suggestions from other location based services. Just yes or no, until you and your friend agree on a place. Enjoy converging!

About

This app aims to cut down on the back and forth when trying to find a place to meet a friend for food and drinks. It takes the geo-locaiton of both individuals, and finds places lcated between those two locations.
