CNVRGE NOW

Find great spots for food...

Halfway between where you and your friends are.

Friend

Want to meet a friend for lunch? Start cnvrge and email or text the link to them. Make sure to allow geolocation on your device.

Location

Once you both join, you will have up to 20 restaurant suggestions to choose from, with the option to click yes or no. If either of you choose no, the option is discarded and you are given another.

Meet

If you both click yes, you'll get the address. Click it to open it in Google maps for directions to the restaurant. That's it! Enjoy.

About

This app was built by Nick Lathe. He is a Seattle based web developer, and saw a need for a simple solution for deciding on a place to meet a friend for lunch. CNVRGE is built on Node.js, and uses AngularJS and Socket.io for a dynamic, real-time front end expereince.
